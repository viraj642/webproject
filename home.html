We Care for Children


Only children believe that they are capable of everything. Our foundation is engaged in improving the lives of underprivileged children by bringing about a positive change in their lives by providing them with necessary education and health facilities. With the support of our volunteers and donors who think of giving not as a duty but as a privilege, we spread many smiles and secure many futures.


Our Mission

We, at DGF, provide underprivileged children with education, ailing people who cants afford treatment with health care and downhearted people with smiles. We make this possible with the help of several individual patrons and corporates who believe in our ideology of “We only have what we give and participate with us in this war to eliminate illiteracy and ill health.
